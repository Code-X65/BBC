About

"We shape our buildings; thereafter they shape us" - Winston Churchil

Black Buyers Consortium was established to to provide clients with a muli-disciplinary Construction Consultancy tailored to their specific needs


With experience in all aspects of the construction industry, we provide professional advice and project management to clients from feasibility to procurement to completion.
At Black Buyers Consortium we pride ourselves on being able to respond to a challenge without pre-fixed ideas or one-fits-all template 'solutions'. Our clients commission us because they know we use sound judgment and will tackle unique or complex tasks by utilising our years of experience and combining our expertise to get the job done.

Black Buyers Consortium founder BlackBuyersConsortium founder and principal, Declan, started his career in the construction industry as a tradesman in his native Dublin working on some of the city's finest historic buildings. He quickly progressed into a management role gaining vast technical and commercial expertise along the way.

Moving to the UK in 2002, Declan worked in Project Management roles for national contractors, working on iconic projects such as London's Shard. His work included residential, educational and commercial projects - both for private, commercial and public sector clients.

In 2013, Declan decided to take the next step and founded Black Buyers Consortium Projects, a client focused construction consultancy providing a unique tailored service offering Project Management, Client's Representative, Contract Administrators and Surveying solutions.

Black Buyers Consortium unparalleled knowledge of the design and construction process along with an empathy with client's requirements and a detailed understanding of the roles and responsibilities of the consultants, contractors and sub-contractors involved, have been proven hugely beneficial to their client's projects.
