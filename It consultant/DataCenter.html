Data Center

The data centre sector continues to grow quickly in terms of size, shape and complexity. Regulation, efficiency expectations, demand and cost are all increasing, development opportunities and availability of competent delivery teams are limited and optimal and often superior levels of both resilience and physical / digital security are expected.

Lysander’s entry into the data centre sector was driven, to a degree by existing clients, as well as a belief that challenges in the sector can be overcome through utilising a combination of our experience from the logistics sector and our construction services provision.

Our Approach

Operating across the UK and Europe, Lysander Data Centre Services provides a full range of professional services to guide data centre and mission-critical projects through permitting, design, construction, operations and post-completion complexities. Our integrated multi-disciplinary services and dedicated director-led teams significantly reduce the risk to data centre owners and operators both during development as well as over the lifetime of the facility.

Our data centre sector specific improvement initiatives include:

  • Simplifying delivery
  • Integrating design standardisation
  • Accelerating design decision making
  • Cost effective sustainability
  • Overcoming regional variances
